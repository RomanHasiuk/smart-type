# 🌐 User Guide: Smart Type Web

Welcome to **Smart Type**! Your intelligent AI assistant for voice dictation, translation, and text processing.

🌐 **Web App:** [https://smart-type-five.vercel.app/](https://smart-type-five.vercel.app/)

This guide will help you set up and get started quickly.

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Getting and Entering Your API Key

The app requires a personal API key from Google. It is completely **free** and takes about 1 minute.

### 📝 Step-by-Step Guide:
1. Go to the official [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com) website.
2. Sign in with your Google (Gmail) account.
3. Click the blue **"Create API key"** button.
4. Copy the generated string (it starts with `AIzaSy...`).
5. Return to Smart Type and paste the key into the **`API Key`** field at the top of the screen.
6. The system saves your key automatically.

> [!TIP]
> Your key is stored locally in your browser. It is private and accessible only to you.

---

## 🤖 Step 2: Choosing an AI Model

Below the **`API Key`** field, you can select an AI model based on your needs:

### ⚡ Fast Models (Lite):
* **`Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite`** and **`Gemini 3.5 Flash Lite`**
  * **Speed:** Very fast (1–2 seconds).
  * **Best for:** Daily dictation, quick notes, messages, and simple translations.

### 🧠 Smart Models (Pro):
* **`Gemini 3.6 Flash`**
  * **Speed:** High performance with deep analysis.
  * **Best for:** Complex tasks, business emails, code analysis, and answering difficult questions.
* **`Gemini 3.5 Flash`**
  * **Note:** Frequently experiences high server demand, which may cause errors or slower response times.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Recommendation:** For daily use, select **`Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite`** or the newer **`Gemini 3.5 Flash Lite`**. For complex tasks, use **`Gemini 3.6 Flash`** for better stability.

---

## 🌍 Step 3: Language, Tone, and Emojis

### 1. Selecting Language
Choose your target language from the list (e.g., *English, Ukrainian, Polish*). If your language isn't listed, simply **type it into the field**.

### 2. Selecting Tone
Choose a style for the AI to apply (e.g., *Neutral, Friendly, Professional, Grammar Fix*). You can also type your own custom style (e.g., *Poetry, Business Letter*).

### 😅 Expressive Emojis:
To have the AI reflect your emotions in text:
* Add **any emoji** to your tone name (e.g., `Casual 🙂`, `Friendly 👍`).
* The AI will analyze your vocal intonation and automatically insert relevant emojis (`😂`, `😅`, `🤔`, `😮`) while dictating.

---

## 🛠️ Step 4: Control Panel

### 🎙️ 1. Dictation
Click the microphone icon to start. Speak naturally; the AI will automatically remove pauses, stuttering, and filler words.

### ❓ 2. Q&A Mode
Toggle between **Standard mode** and **Q&A mode** (using the `❓` / `💬` button) to allow the AI to answer your questions.

### ✨ 3. Magic Wand
Click the `✨` button to process manually entered text, applying your chosen language and tone settings.

### 📋 4. Copy and Clear
* **`📋`**: Copy the text to your clipboard.
* **`🗑️`**: Clear all text fields.
* **`🔊`**: Listen to the translated text using text-to-speech.

---

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Errors:** If a model fails, try switching between `Gemini 3.6 Flash` and `Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite`.
2. **Quality:** For a perfect translation, process the cleaned text again using the `✨` button.
3. **Mic Setup:** Ensure your microphone is working by using the **`Test Mic`** button in the settings.
